Ethylene

This food produces ethylene and can speed up the ripening process of other fruits and vegetables.

Storage

5° C - 10° C

Apples should be stored in the fridge and warmed to room temperature before consumption.

Tips & notes

If possible, keep apples in a box or bag with holes. This way moisture cannot escape as quickly and the apple stays crisp longer.
