Storage

0° C - 5° C

Chicory should be kept in the crisper drawer of the fridge.

Under these conditions, the vegetable keeps for several days.

Tips & notes

Chicory should always be stored in the dark. Too much light can discolor the chicory and it can quickly become bitter.

Wrap the chicory in a damp towel before putting it in the fridge. This way it will stay fresh for longer.
